Problem

Aiding accounting department with calculations and reports which use complex data factors.

My contribution

I were responsible for design and implementation of final report cards, which constituted in multiple, often retrospective factors. I designed it using Chain of responsibility pattern and provided methods for implementation by other team members, which were used in final result. I had also persisted whole result to avoid retroactive parameters change.
